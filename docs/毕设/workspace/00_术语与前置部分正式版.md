# 术语与前置部分正式版

## 1. 正式论文题名

### 中文题名

`Agent驱动的智能视频剪辑系统的设计与实现`

### 英文题名

`Design and Implementation of an Agent-Driven Intelligent Video Editing System`

## 2. 中文摘要

随着短视频创作和个人影像记录需求的持续增长，用户在面对长时视频素材时，普遍存在素材浏览成本高、时间线操作复杂、难以将自然语言目标直接转化为可执行剪辑结果等问题。针对上述问题，本文设计并实现了一个 `Agent（智能体）` 驱动的智能视频剪辑系统 `EntroCut`。本文从视频粗剪任务本质出发，将剪辑过程抽象为 `select（选择）`、`compose（编排）`、`evaluate（评估）` 和 `revise（修订）` 四类核心动作，并以 `EditDraft（剪辑草案）` 作为统一事实源，构建了由素材解析、多模态表示、语义检索、智能规划、局部修订、预览生成与结果导出组成的智能剪辑闭环。系统采用 `client + core + server` 三层协同架构，其中 `core` 作为本地中枢承担项目状态维护、媒体处理与 `Editing Agent（剪辑智能体）` 执行，`server` 负责模型能力代理与语义检索中介，`client` 负责交互呈现与状态同步。本文完成了面向桌面端场景的最小 `MVP（最小可行产品）` 系统实现，并结合典型业务流程对系统运行过程与功能效果进行了分析。结果表明，本文系统能够支持自然语言驱动的视频粗剪、结构化草案生成、局部修订与预览导出，为 `Agent` 驱动智能视频剪辑系统的设计与实现提供了可行方案。

### 中文关键词

`Agent`；智能视频剪辑；自然语言交互；`EditDraft`；多模态检索

## 3. 英文摘要

With the continuous growth of short-video creation and personal media recording, users are increasingly confronted with large volumes of raw footage, high browsing costs, complicated timeline operations, and the difficulty of transforming natural-language goals into executable editing results. To address these problems, this thesis designs and implements `EntroCut`, an `Agent`-driven intelligent video editing system. Starting from the essence of video rough-cut tasks, the editing process is abstracted into four core actions: `select`, `compose`, `evaluate`, and `revise`. An `EditDraft` is introduced as the unified source of truth, around which a complete intelligent editing workflow is built, including media parsing, multimodal representation, semantic retrieval, intelligent planning, local revision, preview generation, and result export. The system adopts a three-layer architecture composed of `client`, `core`, and `server`. Among them, `core` serves as the local execution hub for project-state maintenance, media processing, and `Editing Agent` orchestration; `server` provides model capability proxying and semantic retrieval services; and `client` is responsible for interaction presentation and state synchronization. A desktop-oriented `MVP` system is implemented, and its running process and functional effects are analyzed through a typical workflow. The results show that the system supports natural-language-driven video rough cutting, structured draft generation, local revision, and preview export, providing a practical solution for the design and implementation of `Agent`-driven intelligent video editing systems.

### English Keywords

`Agent`; intelligent video editing; natural language interaction; `EditDraft`; multimodal retrieval

## 4. 术语统一规范

1. 论文正式题名统一为：`Agent驱动的智能视频剪辑系统的设计与实现`。
2. 正文中系统全称统一表述为：`Agent（智能体）` 驱动的智能视频剪辑系统。
3. 核心术语统一为：
   - `Editing Agent（剪辑智能体）`
   - `EditDraft（剪辑草案）`
   - `Agent Planning（智能体规划）`
   - `MVP（最小可行产品）`
   - `local-first（本地优先）`
4. “智能视频剪辑系统”作为总称优先使用；“视频粗剪”用于描述本文聚焦的任务边界。
5. 全文统一使用“本文”“本系统”，不使用“本项目”“本课题”“我们”。
